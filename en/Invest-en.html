Our investment strategy focuses on companies with projectable fundamental value, especially those that have demonstrated a solid track record of more than 20 years in the capital markets.

In order to minimize medium-term volatility, we combine these investments with low exposure to disruptive innovation companies or those with high long-term fundamental value.

This strategy is designed for conservative investors with an investment horizon of at least 2 years and low to moderate risk tolerance.

         

Our investment strategy aims to provide moderate exposure to disruptive innovation through companies with projectable fundamental value in order to minimize volatility in exchange for slightly lower returns.

We focus on companies that focus on areas such as artificial intelligence, robotics, energy storage and DNA sequencing, and combine them with companies that have more predictable and stable revenues to achieve a balance in the portfolio.

This strategy is designed for moderate investors with an investment horizon of at least 5 years and a medium risk tolerance.

         

Our investment strategy focuses on providing broad exposure to disruptive innovation and companies with high fundamental value.

We focus on areas of innovation such as artificial intelligence, robotics, energy storage, DNA sequencing and blockchain technology, which will transform industries and create significant growth in the future.

If you are looking for a bold investment with an investment horizon of at least 5 years and a high risk tolerance, this strategy may be for you.

         

If you are looking for a more stable investment option with lower volatility, we have a fixed income strategy that includes corporate bonds, sovereign debt and dividend funds.

This strategy is designed for investors who seek modest returns and want to minimize risk.

We also combine these funds with others that buy U.S. treasury debt as well as developing country debt to further diversify your portfolio and reduce risk. If you would like to explore this investment option, contact us for more information.

         

All these investments are made for long-term investment purposes, leaving other types of instruments to be invested for horizons of less than 5 years.
